Service Worker Sample: Prefetching Resources During Registration

Available in Chrome 40+

This sample demonstrates basic Service Worker registration, in conjunction with pre-caching of a list of specific resource URLs during the installation phase.

Visit chrome://serviceworker-internals and click on the "Inspect" button next to the registered Service Worker to view logging statements for the various actions the service-worker.js script is performing.
